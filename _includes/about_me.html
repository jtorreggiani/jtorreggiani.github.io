About

I'm on a mission to do the most good I can. Studying history and environmental ethics as an undergraduate impressed upon me the severity of the challenges facing the world, and convinced me that meaningful change requires both understanding systems and building solutions. That conviction led me from political science into intensive self-directed learning in STEM, and ultimately to a career in software.

Over the past 13 years, I've worked across edtech, sustainability, and fintech as an engineer, engineering manager, open-source advocate, and champion for mental health and suicide prevention awareness.

Today I run Integrative R&D, a multidisciplinary consulting practice that helps teams and organizations solve complex technical and organizational challenges. I also lead SPEC, an open learning collective I co-founded that provides diverse communities the resources and opportunities to build skills, develop careers, and become leaders of sustainable social impact.
